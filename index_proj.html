Research Project Involved

  • ANASA: Autonomous robotic system for urban waste recycling [website]
    Partnership Agreement 2014-2020 grant (GA-03110) Sept 2018-Sept 2021.
    ANASA is a Nationally funded endeavor that regards the development and commercialization of a robotic system for the autonomous separation of recyclable items on conveyor belts. Based on input streams from a multitude of sensory devices, object items are detected, categorized and appropriately pick-n-placed.

  • ENTIMENT: Industrial Exploitation and Market Uptake of a Temporal Cognition Toolbox for Commercial Robots [website]
    EU FET-Open-4-2016-2017 grant (GA: 754423) (2017-2019).
    Funded by the very competitive Industrial Exploitation strand of FET, ENTIMENT pursued the commercialization of a modeling toolbox that endows autonomous robots with capacities for long-term interaction and synergies with humans.

  • TIMESTORM - Mind and Time: Investigation of the Temporal Traits of Human-Machine Convergence [website]
    EU FET-Proactive grant (GA: 641100) (2014 - 2017).
    The project investigated the long-term symbiosis and interaction of humans with artificial agents, focusing on processing an abundance of sensory inputs to create models that allow robots to perceive time and spatial constraints, and synchronize their actions and perceptions with those of humans.
    Highlight: Timestorm has recently been declared a Success Story by EU

  • METR: Mental Training in Artificial Agents
    Aristeia II", Ministry of Education, Lifelong Learning and Religious Affairs. National Strategic Reference Framework (NSRF), (Apr 2014-Feb 2015).
    METR was a National project that dealt with the modeling and integration of agent-based partial brain models for robotic systems.
